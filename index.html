FOUNDATION

Sunshine Science School, located in Barzulla Srinagar, Kashmir (190005), was founded in 1992 by the Late Mukhtar Ahmad Dar, who served as its founder and principal. A visionary with a kind heart and deep faith, he was known for his dedication to education and his unwavering support for the underprivileged. He believed in providing quality education to all, regardless of their financial circumstances, and empowered countless students to achieve their dreams.

Late Mukhtar Ahmad Dar was a deeply religious person who not only focused on academic excellence but also instilled moral and spiritual values in his students. He used to teach the Qur'an and other Islamic teachings to his students, ensuring they grew up with a strong foundation in both worldly knowledge and religious principles. Known for his generous spirit and commitment to helping those in need, Late Mukhtar Ahmad Dar was not just a principal but a father figure to his students. His legacy of compassion and service continues to inspire everyone associated with Sunshine Science School.

Our Principal

Since his passing, the role of Principal has been taken over by his wife, Ms. Afshan Firdous, who has carried forward his vision with unwavering determination and care. Her leadership has ensured that the values and principles established by Late Mukhtar Ahmad Dar continue to define the school's mission. Together, both Late Mukhtar Ahmad Dar and Ms. Afshan Firdous have exemplified what it means to be pillars of the community, dedicating their lives to the service of others and fostering a brighter future through education.

Today, Sunshine Science School stands as a testament to our Founder's vision, nurtured by the tireless efforts of his wife, Ms. Afshan Firdous.

Faculty

Throughout the years, the staff of Sunshine Science School has played an integral role in its success. Their dedication, professionalism, and cooperation have greatly contributed to creating an environment of excellence and mutual respect. The collective effort of the staff has ensured that the school continues to thrive and fulfill its mission of shaping bright futures.
